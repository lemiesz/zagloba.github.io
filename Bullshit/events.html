November 9, 2013 Polish Independance Day Party
National Independence Day is a public holiday in Poland celebrated every year on November 11th to commemorate the anniversary of Poland's resumption of independent statehood in 1918 after 123 years of partition by Russia, Prussia and Austria. This year, we will be celebrating 2 days in advance!

Polish Independance Day Party
