Welcome Party

This Friday we will be hosting our first party of the year!!

We will be meeting at the front steps of Foellinger Auditorium at 8PM. There, we will take a club picture for the 2013-2014 school year and walk to the party together.

As this is our opening party, it is FREE for everyone!!! Feel free to invite your friends. Our welcome party is a great place to drink and socialize with other polish and non polish members. It's guaranteed to be an awesome time! There will be a large variety of drinks as well as music and games set up!
